## Highest-risk packaging conflicts (the three to solve first)

1. **E-01 + E-02 + E-03 together** — the real question of the whole project: *does the
   full electronics package physically fit around the central shock and drivetrain, and
   can it be mounted?* No printed structure exists for it yet. **Solve with a dummy-block
   dry-fit (D-01/02/03/04) before committing any mount CAD.**
2. **E-11 (Gate A)** — the rear stack/68 mm-shock articulation gates the entire rear
   group and couples to the wing (E-12). Solvable now via slicer + diagnostic TP prints.
3. **E-07 (camera)** — thermal + FOV + VR roll alignment; start by measuring the real
   camera (D-06) which is on hand.
